Active Runway

Version 1.0

Developer: Lawrence N. Masullo, MD
June 28, 2017

Active Runway is a tool to assist you in determining the most appropriate runway to land on with the least crosswind based on the current winds. You, as the Pilot in Command, have the ultimate responsibility to determine the best course of action based on the current situation. Active Runway can help decrease your workload, but use at your own risk.

Enjoy, and I hope you find this application helpful. I welcome any comments or suggestions. Please feel free to email me at support@activerunway.net
