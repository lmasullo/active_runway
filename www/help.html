Active Runway Help

Choosing the Best Landing Runway

  • Set the runway's heading by sliding the slider or tapping the "+" or "-" buttons
  • Similarly, set the wind direction by sliding the slider or tapping the "+" or "-" buttons
  • Click the "Calculate" button and the program will choose the best runway to land on based on the least crosswind

Airport with Two Runways

  • Click "Prefs." on the bottom navigation bar and it will open a page that allows you to set the number of runways to display
  • Change the flip switch to "Two"
  • Click the "Back" button on the top navigation bar
  • Set the runways' headings and wind direction as described above
  • Click the "Calculate" button and the program will look at all four possible landing headings and choose the best runway to land on based on the least crosswind

Resetting the Form

  • Click "Reset" on the bottom navigation bar and it will reset all the sliders to their default values

Active Runway Preferences

  • Click "Prefs" on the bottom navigation bar and it will open a page that allows you to set the number of runways to display. Your choices are One or Two Runways

About Active Runway

  • Click "About" on the bottom navigation bar and it will open a page that displays the version number, a link to the Active Runway website, and the disclaimer

How to Use Active Runway

  • Click "Help" on the bottom navigation bar and it will open this page!
